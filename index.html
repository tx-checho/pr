What I Learned From My Brochure

My capstone brochure — "Breaking & Entering: A Guide to Penetration Testing" — covered the full landscape of ethical hacking as a career. Here are the key areas it explored:

⚡ What Is Pen Testing?

Penetration testing is the practice of deliberately attempting to break into systems, networks, and applications with the full permission of the owner. The goal isn't destruction — it's discovery.

The techniques are identical to those used by criminals. The only difference is a contract that makes it legal.

🛠 Tools of the Trade
Wireshark Nmap Metasploit Burp Suite John the Ripper

I used Wireshark to analyze live traffic, simulated denial-of-service attacks, and gained hands-on experience exploiting vulnerabilities in Windows and ChromeOS.
